# Style database

Aesthetic directions, each with its token tendencies: surface language, radii, motion feel, and when to reach for it. Pick one direction per project and hold it. Pair with a palette (`palettes.md`) and a type pairing (`type-pairings.md`).

Format: name, when. surface · radii · motion · signature.

## Restraint-led
- **Swiss editorial**: content-first, publishing, agencies. surface flat, hairline separators · radii sharp (2 to 8px) · motion minimal, fast reveals · signature: a strong type scale, generous whitespace, one accent, grid discipline. Pair with a serif display + grotesk body.
- **Swiss international (rationalist)**: institutions, foundations, archives. surface flat, white-dominant, mathematical grid · radii sharp (0 to 4px) · motion near-none, instant · signature: Helvetica or Neue Haas Grotesk, a strict modular grid, flush-left ragged-right text, red the only permitted accent, objectivity as the look. Stricter and colder than Swiss editorial. Pair Akzidenz-Grotesk display + the same family body.
- **Minimal product**: clean SaaS, tools. surface flat or soft · radii 8 to 12px · motion subtle, ease-out · signature: near-monochrome + one accent, restraint, clarity over decoration.
- **Warm minimal**: calm consumer, beauty. surface soft, low contrast · radii 12 to 20px · motion gentle · signature: cream/blush neutrals, one warm accent, soft and inviting. Less clinical than minimal product, the air feels lived-in.
- **Quiet luxury**: premium brands, boutique. surface soft shadow · radii 4 to 14px · motion slow, considered · signature: a metallic hairline, a serif, lots of air, muted tuned neutrals, never loud.
- **Soft pastel**: wellness, parenting, gentle consumer, journaling. surface flat washed tints · radii 16 to 28px · motion slow, soft ease · signature: desaturated mint/lilac/peach neutrals on warm off-white, one slightly deeper tint as accent, rounded everything, calm over energy. Guard contrast so text still passes; pastels love to go illegible.
- **Monochrome**: photography, portfolios, fashion, austere brands. surface flat, single hue or pure grayscale · radii 4 to 10px · motion minimal · signature: zero chromatic accent, hierarchy built entirely from weight, size, and value, the image or the type carries all the color there is. Discipline is the whole point.
- **Duotone**: music, festivals, bold editorial, campaign microsites. surface flat, two-color image treatment · radii 0 to 8px · motion sharp, rationed · signature: photography mapped to two committed hues (a deep shadow tone and a bright highlight tone), type in one of the two, a recognizable poster-grade signature. Pick the pair on purpose, never default cyan/magenta unless it is the brand.

## Material / depth-led
- **Liquid glass**: Apple-grade apps, premium dark. surface translucent glass (`backdrop-filter` blur + saturate + inner sheen, see `glass.md`) over a colored halo · radii 16 to 24px · motion fluid springs, floating · signature: light makes the depth, not borders. Earn it with the brand; do not use everywhere.
- **Glassmorphism (frosted panels)**: overlays, modals, music and weather UIs, dashboards over imagery. surface frosted translucent panels (blur + 8 to 14% white fill + a 1px light hairline) floating over a colored or photographic background · radii 14 to 22px · motion soft fades, gentle lift · signature: legibility comes from the blur and a subtle border, not opacity alone. Always set a solid fallback and test text contrast against the busiest background behind it.
- **Soft elevation**: friendly apps, dashboards. surface solid cards + soft shadow (`0 4px 18px rgba(0,0,0,.12)`) · radii 14 to 22px · motion spring micro-interactions · signature: gentle depth, rounded, tactile.
- **Glossy dimensional**: playful, gaming, kids. surface rich gradients + highlights · radii 18 to 28px · motion bouncy springs (rationed) · signature: 3D-ish icons, vivid, delight. Keep typography clean to balance.

## Structure / raw-led
- **Brutalist**: statements, raw, anti-template. surface flat, hard 1px borders · radii 0 to 4px · motion abrupt or none, linear · signature: stark contrast, a heavy or mono type, hi-vis accent, visible grid, no soft edges.
- **Brutalist refined**: design studios, fashion, confident portfolios. surface flat, hairline borders, oversized type · radii 0 to 2px · motion deliberate, a single hard cut · signature: brutalism's raw bones with editorial taste, a huge grotesk set tight, asymmetric tension held by a real grid, black on off-white with one severe accent. Restraint is what separates it from the noisy kind.
- **Industrial / utility**: B2B, engineering, data. surface flat, functional · radii 4 to 8px · motion functional, fast · signature: dense information allowed, safety-color accent, tabular numbers, no flourish.
- **Industrial utilitarian (workwear)**: logistics, manufacturing, tools, trades. surface flat, off-white or concrete gray, boxed sections · radii 0 to 4px · motion none to minimal · signature: a condensed grotesk, stencil or label cues, hazard-tape yellow or orange rationed, dividers and part-number energy, nothing decorative survives. Honest and heavy. Pair a condensed display + a plain grotesk body.
- **Blueprint / technical**: architecture, engineering firms, hardware, technical docs. surface deep blueprint blue or off-white drafting paper, fine grid · radii 0 to 2px · motion none, instant · signature: thin cyan or white linework, dimension lines and callouts, a mono or drafting-style type, measured and precise, the page reads like a drawing. One ink color, no fills.
- **Geometric / Bauhaus**: bold agencies, posters. surface flat, blocks of color · radii 0 to 8px · motion sharp, staggered · signature: primary colors, circles/squares/triangles, asymmetry.
- **Memphis geometric**: youth brands, events, playful tech, the 80s done knowingly. surface flat, busy with shapes and squiggles · radii mixed, 0 and fully round together · motion bouncy, staggered, a little chaotic · signature: clashing-but-curated brights, terrazzo and squiggle motifs, dropped grids, joyful disorder held by one disciplined neutral and a tight type. Fun, not random; over-applied it turns to noise fast.

## Warm / organic-led
- **Organic editorial**: wellness, food, craft, architecture. surface flat with stone/paper warmth · radii 6 to 16px · motion soft, slow · signature: warm neutrals, earthy accent, real photography over illustration, generous margins.
- **Organic biophilic**: sustainability, skincare, garden and plant brands, slow food. surface flat with botanical photography and natural texture · radii 10 to 20px, often leaf-soft · motion slow, breathing, eased · signature: sage/clay/moss earth palette, a humanist serif or soft grotesk, real foliage and grain, lots of negative space, daylight not studio light. Calm and grown, never corporate green.
- **Paper and ink editorial**: essays, journals, literary sites, considered blogs. surface warm off-white paper, ink-dark text · radii 0 to 4px · motion minimal, the page turns quietly · signature: a refined book serif at a true reading measure, drop caps or small caps allowed, ink-black on cream, footnotes and marginalia, the whole thing reads like a fine printed page. Typography is the entire design.
- **Hand-drawn / sketch**: indie products, education, kids, personal brands, changelogs with character. surface flat off-white, hand-rendered marks · radii loose and irregular · motion slightly imperfect, hand-timed · signature: a marker or felt-tip display, doodle arrows and underlines, rough-edged shapes, a warm paper tone, one bright marker accent. Charm by intent; keep body copy a clean typeface so it stays readable.
- **Scrapbook / collage**: culture, music zines, lookbooks, community projects. surface layered cut-paper, tape, photo corners · radii torn and irregular · motion slight tilt and settle · signature: mixed photography and cutouts, masking tape and staple cues, rotated elements, handwritten and printed type together, a warm aged ground. Curated mess; a strict grid underneath keeps it from collapsing.

## Dark / premium-led
- **Dark gold (lacquer)**: premium nocturnal, fintech, luxury. surface near-black + a metallic accent + subtle texture · radii 10 to 18px · motion smooth, restrained · signature: gold or amber on tuned near-black, champagne text, a sense of weight.
- **Dark luxury**: high jewelry, spirits, members-only, premium automotive. surface deep near-black with a faint warm or cool undertone, generous void · radii 2 to 10px · motion very slow, weighted, nothing hurries · signature: a high-contrast display serif, hairline metallic rules, enormous negative space, one restrained metal accent (champagne, platinum, or bronze), photography lit like a still life. Silence and space signal the price tag, not gloss.
- **Dark techy**: dev tools, infra, crypto. surface near-black + a single vivid cool accent · radii 8 to 14px · motion precise, fast · signature: mono labels, glowing accent rationed, terminal-adjacent confidence (never neon-everything).

## Expressive / statement-led
- **Editorial maximalism**: bold brand, fashion, culture. surface flat, layered type and image · radii 0 to 8px · motion staggered expressive reveals · signature: huge mixed-size headlines, overlap, a strong point of view, image-led. The disciplined opposite of slop: maximal but intentional.
- **Bold maximalist editorial**: fashion houses, magazines, statement agencies, awards-bait. surface flat, full-bleed, type and image colliding on purpose · radii 0 to 4px · motion big staggered reveals, generous scroll choreography · signature: a serif display pushed to absurd sizes, color-blocked sections, deliberate overlap and clipping, a loud committed palette, every screen a spread. Louder than editorial maximalism and proud of it; one strict grid keeps it from being slop.
- **Retro-futurism / Y2K**: nostalgic tech, music, youth. surface chrome and gradient highlights · radii 8 to 20px · motion glossy, rationed · signature: chrome type, holographic accent, mono or pixel cues. Keep one nostalgic move, not all of them.
- **Tasteful Y2K**: youth fashion, streetwear, music drops, gen-z brands. surface flat with chrome and bubble accents on clean white or silver · radii fully round on pills, sharp elsewhere · motion snappy, a little springy · signature: a chrome or gel logo, lime/cyber-blue/hot-pink accent rationed to one, pixel or bubble type as a spice not the body, sparkle cues used once. The 2002 reference filtered through current restraint, not a costume.
- **Vaporwave (tasteful)**: music, net-art, nightlife, experimental microsites. surface deep gradient dusk (teal to magenta) with grid horizon · radii 4 to 16px · motion slow drift, subtle glow · signature: a sunset gradient, a single classical-statue or grid motif, cyan-and-pink duotone imagery, a wide retro type used sparingly, melancholy not parody. One nostalgic device, held; pile them up and it becomes a meme.
- **Art deco / geometric luxe**: heritage glam, spirits, events. surface flat with metallic linework · radii 0 to 8px · motion elegant, slow · signature: symmetry, gold hairline motifs (fans, sunbursts), high contrast, a display serif.
- **Risograph print**: indie publishers, posters, events, illustration-led. surface warm paper with grainy single-pass color · radii 0 to 6px · motion minimal, a slight registration jump allowed · signature: two or three spot inks (fluoro pink, blue, yellow) that overprint into a third, visible grain and misregistration, flat illustration, a chunky grotesk. The imperfection is the charm; fake the overprint with multiply blends and a grain texture.
- **Newsprint**: journalism, culture commentary, op-ed, broadsheet revivals. surface warm gray paper stock · radii 0 · motion none, instant · signature: a true newspaper masthead serif, multi-column justified text with rules between, a small condensed grotesk for kickers and bylines, halftone imagery, dateline energy. Black ink and paper, with at most one spot color for a section.
- **Organic blob / soft shapes**: friendly modern, consumer, kids-adjacent. surface flat with blob masks and soft shapes · radii organic · motion gentle morphing · signature: rounded organic forms, a soft committed palette, approachable without being childish.

## Material / depth-led (more)
- **Aurora / gradient mesh**: modern hero, AI, music, done right. surface a soft drifting mesh-gradient halo behind flat, legible content · radii 12 to 20px · motion slow drifting blobs (reduced-motion freezes them) · signature: an ambient colored field is the only flourish; content stays flat and readable. Commit to a non-purple palette so it does not read as default AI.
- **Claymorphism**: playful apps, kids, games. surface inflated soft inner and outer shadows · radii 20 to 32px · motion bouncy but gentle · signature: puffy pastel shapes, tactile, delight. Keep type clean to balance.
- **Neumorphism**: accents only, never whole UI. surface same-color extruded soft shadows · radii 14 to 24px · motion subtle · signature: monochrome tactile. Warning: low contrast by nature, fails accessibility for text and primary controls; use for a toggle or a card detail, not the interface.

## Structure / raw-led (more)
- **Neo-brutalist**: playful-bold brands, indie products. surface flat with hard offset shadows (`4px 4px 0`) · radii 0 to 6px · motion snappy, no fancy easing · signature: thick black borders, bright flat fills, sticker energy. Brutalist's fun cousin, not its austere one.
- **Editorial grid / magazine**: publishing, portfolios, agencies. surface flat with visible columns and hairline rules · radii 0 to 6px · motion minimal · signature: multi-column layout, captions, asymmetric balance, a strong baseline grid.
- **Magazine (cover-led)**: culture sites, fashion, long-form features. surface flat with a hero spread and pull quotes · radii 0 to 6px · motion editorial reveals, considered scroll · signature: a cover-story opener, oversized display serif headlines, deck and byline hierarchy, pull quotes that break the column, image captions, generous gutters. Reads like an issue, not a feed. Pair a high-contrast serif display + a clean grotesk body.
- **Monospace / technical**: dev tools, data, security. surface flat with grid lines · radii 2 to 8px · motion precise and fast · signature: a mono display or labels, tabular numbers, terminal cues, color rationed hard.
- **Terminal / monospace UI**: CLIs, developer dashboards, security, infra status. surface near-black or paper-white, the whole interface in one mono family · radii 0 to 4px · motion instant, a blinking caret, no easing theatrics · signature: everything monospaced including body, a green or amber single accent (or none), ASCII rules and box-drawing, prompt and cursor cues, content snaps to a character grid. Type is the entire system; one accent, used like a status light.

## Dark / premium-led (more)
- **Cinematic dark**: entertainment, media, film, launches. surface deep dark with full-bleed imagery · radii 8 to 16px · motion smooth fades, parallax rationed · signature: media bleeds edge to edge, dramatic contrast, light comes from the content, UI recedes.
- **Neon noir**: nightlife, gaming, esports, edgy launches. surface near-black with a single saturated glow · radii 6 to 14px · motion sharp, a controlled pulse · signature: one electric accent (cyan, magenta, or acid) used as a light source with a tight bloom, deep shadows, a condensed or mono display, rim-lit imagery. Discipline is everything; two neons and it reads as a casino. Ration the glow to focal points.
- **Gradient mesh aurora (nocturnal)**: AI products, music, dark heroes done right. surface deep near-black with a slow drifting mesh-gradient aurora behind flat content · radii 12 to 20px · motion gentle blob drift, frozen under reduced-motion · signature: a luminous colored field as the only flourish, content stays flat and legible above it, an ambient glow rather than gradient buttons. Commit to a distinctive non-purple pairing (teal to amber, rose to gold) so it never reads as default AI.

## Accessibility-led
- **High-contrast clear**: civic, gov, healthcare, a11y-first brands. surface flat with strong borders · radii 6 to 12px · motion minimal, reduced-motion as the baseline · signature: AAA contrast, large hit targets, an unmissable focus ring, no ambiguity. Constraint as an aesthetic, and the safest default when the audience is broad.
- **High-contrast accessible (expressive)**: public services with a brand, education, fintech that must pass audit. surface flat, light or dark, deliberate borders · radii 8 to 14px · motion subtle, reduced-motion respected · signature: AA-plus contrast on every text and control, a bold confident accent that still clears 4.5:1, focus visible everywhere, color never the only signal. Proof that accessible does not mean bland; the constraint shapes a strong, legible look.

## Niche / spice-led
- **Pixel / 8-bit**: retro games, indie devs, playful 404s and easter eggs. surface flat, hard-edged, snapped to a pixel grid · radii 0 · motion stepped, no anti-aliasing · signature: a bitmap display type, a limited indexed palette, chunky pixel icons and borders, dithering instead of gradients. A whole-site commitment for game brands, or a single delightful corner elsewhere; pair the pixel face with a clean readable body so nobody squints.
- **Type-only manifesto**: writers, critics, statements, single-page launches. surface stark off-white or near-black, no imagery at all · radii 0 · motion none · signature: one oversized grotesk doing everything, raw left-aligned blocks, honest default underlines on links, zero decoration, the argument carries the page. The most extreme restraint in this file; the words are the whole design.
- **Sticker / pop**: consumer apps, creator tools, fun commerce. surface flat bright fills with thick outlines · radii 12 to 24px · motion bouncy, a satisfying pop · signature: sticker-style shapes with a heavy contour and a small drop, saturated candy palette held by one neutral, rounded chunky type, playful icons. Joyful and tactile; keep one section calm so the energy lands.
- **Editorial color-block**: campaigns, reports, brand microsites, decks-as-sites. surface large flat panels of committed color, edge to edge · radii 0 to 8px · motion sections slide and snap on scroll · signature: each section a full-bleed color field, type sized to fill it, a tight palette of three or four blocks that hand off cleanly, image and text sharing the block. Bold and ordered; the grid and the limited palette keep it from shouting.

## Picking and holding
- One surface language, one radii scale, one accent across the whole system. No mid-page shifts.
- The style sets the tendencies; the palette and type pairing make it specific. Two clients can share a style but must differ in palette and type.
- Glass and glossy are expensive in attention and GPU. Default to flat or soft unless the brand earns more.
